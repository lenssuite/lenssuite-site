Privacy policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

The short version

Lens Suite extensions (MacroLens, EarningsLens, SectorLens) do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything runs locally in your browser.

What data we collect

None. Our extensions do not require accounts, do not use analytics, do not set cookies, and do not send any data to our servers or any third party. There are no servers — the extensions run entirely in your browser.

What data the extensions access

Our extensions fetch publicly available financial data from Yahoo Finance (query1.finance.yahoo.com and query2.finance.yahoo.com) to calculate scores. This data is market prices, not personal information. The fetched data is stored only in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and is never transmitted elsewhere.

PRO payments

If you choose to upgrade to PRO, payments are processed by ExtensionPay (extensionpay.com), which uses Stripe for secure payment processing. We never see or store your credit card information. ExtensionPay stores only your email address to manage your subscription. See ExtensionPay's privacy policy and Stripe's privacy policy for details.

Chrome Web Store permissions

Our extensions request the following permissions: alarms (to schedule data refreshes), storage (to save scores locally), notifications (to alert you of regime changes). We also request host permissions for Yahoo Finance domains (to fetch market data) and extensionpay.com (for optional PRO payments).

This website

This website (lenssuite.com) does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking. If you submit your email in the notification form, it is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is not transmitted to any server.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how data is handled, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.
